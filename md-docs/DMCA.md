# DMCA Policy

## Spydr DMCA Notice and Takedown Policy

Spydr respects the intellectual property rights of others and expects all users, contributors, and fork maintainers to do the same.

If you believe that content hosted within this repository infringes upon your copyright, you may submit a Digital Millennium Copyright Act (DMCA) takedown request.

## Filing a DMCA Complaint

Your DMCA notice must include the following information:

1. A physical or electronic signature of the copyright owner or a person authorized to act on their behalf.
2. Identification of the copyrighted work claimed to have been infringed.
3. Identification of the allegedly infringing material and sufficient information to locate it.
4. Your contact information, including:

   * Full Name
   * Email Address
   * Organization (if applicable)
5. A statement that you have a good faith belief that the use of the material is not authorized by the copyright owner, its agent, or the law.
6. A statement that the information provided is accurate and, under penalty of perjury, that you are authorized to act on behalf of the copyright owner.

## Submission

DMCA notices should be submitted to:

**Email:** [help.solargames@gmail.com](mailto:help.solargames@gmail.com)

If no dedicated DMCA email exists, notices may be submitted through the project's official contact channels listed in this repository.

## Counter-Notification

If you believe material was removed or disabled as a result of mistake or misidentification, you may submit a counter-notification containing:

1. Your physical or electronic signature.
2. Identification of the material that was removed or disabled and its previous location.
3. A statement under penalty of perjury that you have a good faith belief the material was removed by mistake or misidentification.
4. Your name, address, telephone number, and email address.
5. A statement consenting to the jurisdiction of the appropriate federal court.

## Repeat Infringers

Spydr reserves the right to restrict, remove, or terminate access to content or contributors who repeatedly infringe upon the intellectual property rights of others.

## False Claims

Knowingly submitting false DMCA notices or counter-notifications may result in legal liability. Please ensure all claims are accurate before submission.
